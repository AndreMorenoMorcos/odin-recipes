Burger Recipe

Burger PNGs by Vecteezy

Description

This is a simple recipe for a delicious burger. It includes all the necessary ingredients and steps to make a perfect burger.

Ingredients

  • 100 grams ground beef
  • 1 hamburger bun
  • 2 slices of american cheese
  • 1 tomato, sliced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Ketchup, mustard, and mayonnaise
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill or pan to high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into a patty.
  3. Pan-fry the patty, add salt and pepper to taste. Fry for one minute and a half for each side.
  4. Place a slice of cheese on top of the patty and let it melt.
  5. Toast the hamburger buns lightly if desired.
  6. Assemble the burger by placing the patty on the bottom bun, followed by tomato slices, onion slices, lettuce leaves, and sauces.
  7. Top with the top bun and serve immediately.
