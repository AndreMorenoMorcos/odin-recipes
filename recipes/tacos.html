Tacos Recipe

Description

This is a simple recipe for a delicious taco. It includes all the necessary ingredients and steps to make a perfect taco.

Ingredients

  • 100 grams ground beef
  • 2 taco shells
  • 1 tomato, diced
  • 1 onion, diced
  • Lettuce leaves
  • Shredded cheese
  • Sour cream
  • Salsa
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the grill or pan to high heat.
  2. Form the ground beef into small taco-sized portions.
  3. Pan-fry the beef, add salt and pepper to taste. Fry for one minute and a half for each side until fully cooked.
  4. Warm the taco shells according to the package instructions.
  5. Assemble the tacos by placing the cooked beef in the taco shells, followed by diced tomato, diced onion, lettuce leaves, shredded cheese, sour cream, and salsa.
  6. Serve immediately and enjoy your delicious tacos!
